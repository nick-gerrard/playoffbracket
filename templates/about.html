How it works

A bracket prediction game built for friends. Pick every playoff series, score points as results come in, and settle debates with DucksBucks.

01

Make your picks

Before the first puck drops, predict the winner of every series — all four rounds. Picks lock when the playoffs begin, so choose wisely.

02

Score points as results come in

Points increase each round — a correct call in the Conference Finals is worth more than a first-round pick. Your bracket page tracks every correct call, every miss, and how many points you can still earn.

03

Compete on the leaderboard

See how you stack up in real time. Click any name on the leaderboard to compare brackets side-by-side — find out exactly where you agree, and where you don't.

04

Become a legend

The player with the best bracket at the end of the Stanley Cup Playoffs earns the most coveted prize in the group chat: being right.

DucksBucks

The side game

The bracket settles who's best overall — DucksBucks settle individual arguments. On any game day, challenge a friend: pick the winner, name your wager, and wait for them to accept. Both sides are held in escrow and the pot is automatically paid out the morning after the game.
